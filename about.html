objects with
quiet intention

ÆTHERIC was born from a slow reverence for craft, earth, and the poetry of everyday rituals. We travel to small ateliers, family-run ceramics studios, and ethical textile cooperatives — curating pieces that feel both timeless and alive.

Our name echoes the invisible essence that connects material and soul: the aether within each handmade object.

slow curation

We honor the maker's hand. Each piece passes through a thoughtful lens — prioritizing ethical production, low waste, and natural materials.

earth first

From packaging to pigments, we choose compostable, recycled, or upcycled materials. Over 92% of our collection is plastic-free.

fair kinship

We partner with artisan groups that guarantee fair wages, safe spaces, and intergenerational craft preservation.

the origin story

Founder Elara Voss, a former textile researcher, started ÆTHERIC after noticing the disconnect between mass production and authentic connection. What began as a small studio sale in Copenhagen evolved into a global community of slow-living advocates.

Today, we collaborate with 14 independent makers across 6 countries — from Japanese indigo dyers to Portuguese cork artisans. Every object is chosen to age gracefully, become heirloom, and spark daily wonder.

— Elara Voss, creative director
